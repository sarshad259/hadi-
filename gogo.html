Plant Care Tips for Beginners

For beginners, we advise starting with easy plants as you might not have experience in vertical farming.

List of Easy Plants to Grow:

Herbs

  1. Basil:

    Grows well in pots and loves sunlight.

  2. Mint:

    Hardy and spreads easily; can be invasive in gardens.

  3. Chives:

    A perennial herb that is very easy to grow and maintain.

Outdoor Plants

  1. Marigold:

    Bright and easy to care for; attracts beneficial insects.

  2. Sunflower:

    Grows quickly and thrives in full sun.

  3. Lavender:

    Drought-tolerant and fragrant; prefers sunny spots.

  4. Succulents:

    Low maintenance and require little water.

Indoor Plants

  1. Pothos:

    Tolerates low light and requires minimal care.

  2. Snake Plant:

    Very hardy and thrives in various conditions.

  3. Spider Plant:

    Grows well in indirect light and is resilient.

  4. ZZ Plant:

    Low water requirements and adapts to low light.
