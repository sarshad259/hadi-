Moringa Sea Buckthorn Rooibos Cranberries Blue Lotus Herbs

  1. Moringa

    Growing Conditions:

    Prefers well-drained soil and full sun. Tolerates drought but benefits from regular watering.

    Propagation:

    Can be grown from seeds or cuttings. Seeds need soaking for a day before planting.

    Care:

    Fertilize occasionally; prune to promote bushiness and prevent woodiness.

  2. Sea Buckthorn

    Growing Conditions:

    Thrives in sandy well-drained soils and full sun. Tolerates poor soil and drought.

    Propagation:

    Grown from seeds and cuttings; female plants produce berries, so both genders are needed for fruit.

    Care:

    Requires minimal maintenance, but regular pruning helps maintain shape.

  3. Rooibos

    Growing Conditions:

    Prefers well-drained sandy soil and full sun; drought-tolerant.

    Benefits:

    Rich in antioxidants and caffeine-free.

  4. Cranberries

    Growing Conditions:

    Prefers acidic, sandy soil and wet conditions; can be grown in raised beds.

    Benefits:

    Supports urinary tract health and is high in vitamin C.

  5. Blue Lotus

    Benefits:

    Can be grown in ponds or water gardens with full sun.

    Benefits:

    Known for its calming effects and beautiful flowers.

  6. Herbs (like Basil or Oregano)

    Growing Conditions:

    Easy to grow in pots or gardens; prefer well-drained soil and full sun.

    Benefits:

    Enhance culinary dishes and have medicinal properties.
